## How to Use

1. **Find Your Industry**: Navigate to your sector above
2. **Choose a Palette**: Select by use case (e.g., "Analytics, SaaS dashboards")
3. **Extract Colors**:
   - Primary: Brand, primary buttons, headers
   - Secondary: Supporting elements, accents
   - Neutrals: (Generated per palette) Text, backgrounds, borders
4. **Add Semantics**:
   - Green: Success, positive, growth
   - Red: Danger, error, loss
   - Amber/Orange: Warning, caution
   - Blue: Info, neutral, trust
5. **Dark Mode**: Invert luminosity while maintaining hue and saturation
