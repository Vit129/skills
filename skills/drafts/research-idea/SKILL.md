# research-idea — Structured Research & Idea Validation

**Scope:** Formalize research questions, validate investment ideas, and synthesize findings across lanes (news, ETF, portfolio fit, fundamentals).

**When to use:**
- Validating a new investment thesis or idea
- Researching an ETF or sector before adding to portfolio
- Synthesizing market news into portfolio impact assessment
- Cross-lane analysis (fundamental + news + ETF lens)

**Methodology:**
1. **Scope** — Define research question and lanes (news/fundamentals/ETF/portfolio)
2. **Evidence** — Gather sources with dates and credibility markers
3. **Synthesis** — Build findings across lanes, flag unknowns (N/A if unclear)
4. **Gate** — Cross-check against portfolio constraints (risk, allocation, rebalance cadence)
5. **Decision** — Main agent (Claude Code) owns final investment call, not research skill

**Integration:**
- Part of `portfolio-etf-analysis` skill ecosystem
- Works with finance subagents (news lane, ETF lens, portfolio-fit lane)
- Bounded to evidence gathering; Claude owns final trade decision
