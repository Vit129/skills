### Cardio (Post-Strength)
- **Treadmill:** 2.5 km/h, incline alternating 12/15 (max), every 2 min × 40 min (Z2 walking)

### Biomechanics Notes (from movement-and-load.md)
- DB RDL: ✅ Hip hinge = safe for right hip (no ER forced). Bilateral DB load = more forgiving than barbell for LLD. Good hamstring/glute stimulus
- DB Row Hammer 90°: ✅ Neutral shoulder position = right shoulder safe. 90° elbow = shorter lever, less lat stretch but MORE posterior delt + rhomboid + trap activation
- **Muscle activation profile (Hammer 90° both sets):**
  - Posterior delt: ~82% (high)
  - Rhomboids/Trap: ~78–80% (high)
  - Lats: ~68% (moderate — less stretch than pronated standard)
  - Biceps: ~72% (higher than pronated)
- **What's missing:** Lat stretch/width stimulus. No pronated standard row in this session = less lat activation overall
- **Recommendation next back session:** Add 1–2 sets pronated standard (wider ROM, 60–75° elbow) to balance lat vs posterior chain
