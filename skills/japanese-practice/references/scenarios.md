# Japanese Practice — Conversation Scenarios

Pick one; I'll open with a native-speaker line and you respond in Japanese.

- **Ordering at a restaurant** — order, ask about ingredients/price/wait time
- **Tech interview (日本語面接)** — describe your background, skills, projects
- **Daily conversation with a friend** — describe your week, interests, plans
- **Asking for directions** — understand directions, ask follow-ups
- **Customer service** — ask about return policy, sizes, shipping
